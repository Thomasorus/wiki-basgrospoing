EN TRAVAUX

Trish

TBW

In a nutshell A wide array of projectiles and traps and amazing air mobility makes her a rather unique character. Although her strengths point to keep away, she really shines with her fast tri-dash and big hitboxes on her sword. Still, her damage leaves much to be desired which can be mitigated by her round harvest resets. Using space control assists to cover her projectile recovery is essential and will make her a truly formidable character.

Players to Watch

KBrad, GreenAce, Disgruntled GOA, Bubblan
