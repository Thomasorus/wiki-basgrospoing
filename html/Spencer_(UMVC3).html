Spencer (UMVC3)

EN TRAVAUX

Spencer

N/A

In a nutshell Spencer is one of the few “viable” characters in UMvC3. He is a fantastic anchor with great hypers, great DHC potential, and is a fantastic person to use lvl 3 X Factor on. He has some not too hard to do combos and has great mobility. His assists, however, are kind of lacking. If you want a heavy hitting anchor with great mobility and one of the best hypers in the game, use Spencer.

Players to Watch

Combofiend, Danke, Bee, Knives, Yipes, Nemo, Infrit, Adelheid Stark, Darryn
